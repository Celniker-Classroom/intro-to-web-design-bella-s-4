First part of my life

Isabella Song was born at a very young age to Hsin-Ju Chen and Samuel Xin Song on August 14 of 2009. She is their only child and is a second generation Chinese immigrant. When she was eight years old, her parents got her a dog for her ninth birthday. The dog was named Lily, and soon after, the family moved to Carmel Valley, where Isabella attended sixth grade. Isabella introduced her family nickname “Bella” to her school environment with this change.

Second part of my life

Bella got another dog by the name of Mimi from a family friend after her move to Carmel Valley. She attended Pacific Trails Middle School in her seventh grade. Now, she has two dogs, Lily, and Mimi. Bella is currently a junior at Canyon Crest Academy, and takes AP Computer Science Principles. She is planning on majoring in something related to medicine when she applies for college next year.

Fun Facts

When she was four years old, Bella almost drowned in a pool at her preschool friend’s birthday party. Bella is on the CCA badminton team. If Bella could choose to have a sibling, she would want an older sister. Despite having two dogs, Bella is actually a little bit more of a cat person. Bella has gotten COVID-19 four times after the height of the pandemic. Bella is allergic to both dogs and cats.
